Transclude example

Transclude makes the contents of a directive with this option have access to the scope outside of the directive rather than inside.

return { restrict:'E', transclude:true, template:"<div><input> ng-model='Details.Address'/><div> ng-transclude></div></div>" };
The Address is : {{Details.Address}}
